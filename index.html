About

My name is Shirley. I am a graduate of The Tech Academy's UX/UI Design Boot Camp, trained and experienced in the following web and programming languages: HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

I love the art of design! Eversince I was a child, I enjoyed anything that allowed me to express my creativity. After spending sometime as a hairstylist & makeup
artist in Austin, TX, I moved to Portland on a search for more. I still wanted to connect with others while staying true to my artistic light, and that's how I landed in UI/UX Design.

On my free time, I enjoy creating beauty content, hiking, and working out. Personal development and mental health is something I carry dear to my heart. I am always looking for ways of contributing to self-improvement along with inspiring people to tap into their creativity.

Let's chat! I am always looking to build my portfolio and make beautiful connections!
